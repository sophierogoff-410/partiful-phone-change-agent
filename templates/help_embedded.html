Can I change my phone number?

If you have access to your old number:

  • Open this link to edit your profile
  • Click on your phone number
  • Follow the steps to update your number!

If you don't have access to your old number, don't worry — we can still update it for you once we verify your identity another way.

Reach out to hello@partiful.com if you have further questions.

Takes about a minute — no email required.
